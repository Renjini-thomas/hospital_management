    Emergency Department (ED)

    Overview: The emergency department handles patients with acute illnesses or injuries that require immediate medical attention. It operates 24/7 to ensure critical care for emergencies.
    Services: Trauma care, resuscitation, treatment of severe medical conditions, emergency surgeries, and initial assessments.
    Specialties: Trauma care, cardiology (heart emergencies), neurology (stroke care), and toxicology.

    Cardiology Department

    Overview: This department deals with the diagnosis and treatment of heart-related diseases.
    Services: Electrocardiograms (ECG), stress tests, echocardiograms, cardiac catheterization, angioplasty, heart failure management.
    Specialties: Interventional cardiology, pediatric cardiology, electrophysiology, and cardiac rehabilitation.

    Orthopedics Department

    Overview: Orthopedics focuses on diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal system disorders, including bones, joints, muscles, and ligaments.
    Services: Fracture care, joint replacement surgeries, spinal surgeries, arthroscopy, physical therapy, and rehabilitation.
    Specialties: Sports medicine, pediatric orthopedics, hand surgery, and joint replacement.

    Neurology Department

    Overview: Neurology involves diagnosing and treating diseases of the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.
    Services:EEG (electroencephalogram), MRI/CT scans for the brain, stroke treatment, epilepsy management, and neurorehabilitation.
    Specialties: Stroke care, epilepsy, sleep disorders, movement disorders, and neurodegenerative diseases like Alzheimer's.

    Pediatrics Department

    Overview: The pediatrics department focuses on the medical care of infants, children, and adolescents.
    Services: Well-child visits, vaccinations, pediatric surgeries, treatment of common illnesses, growth and development monitoring.
    Specialties: Neonatology, pediatric surgery, pediatric cardiology, and pediatric endocrinology..

    Obstetrics and Gynecology Department

    Overview: The Obstetrics and Gynecology department provides comprehensive care for women’s health at every stage of life, with a focus on personalized and compassionate care.
    Services: Prenatal and postnatal care ,Labor and delivery, including high-risk pregnancy management Family planning and contraceptive counseling, Treatment of menstrual disorders and hormonal imbalances, Gynecological surgeries and cancer screenings
    Specialties: Maternity care, Reproductive health, Minimally invasive gynecological surgeries, High-risk pregnancy management, Gynecological oncology

    Oncology Department

    Overview: he Oncology department is dedicated to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of cancer, providing advanced medical care with a patient-centered approach
    Services: Cancer screening and early detection, Chemotherapy and radiation therapy, Tumor removal surgeries, Symptom management and palliative care, Comprehensive support for patients and families
    Specialties: Surgical oncology Radiation oncology, Chemotherapy and targeted therapies, Palliative care and pain management, Cancer prevention and awareness programs

    Radiology and Imaging Department

    Overview: The Radiology and Imaging department provides advanced diagnostic imaging services to support accurate medical diagnoses and treatment planning.
    Services: X-rays and ultrasounds CT scans, MRIs, and PET scans Image-guided procedures (e.g., biopsies, catheter placements) Breast imaging and mammography Emergency imaging for trauma cases
    Specialties: Diagnostic radiology Interventional radiology Advanced imaging techniques Emergency imaging services Cancer imaging and detection
